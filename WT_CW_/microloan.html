Microloan for any purpose

  • Purpose of the loan: for any purpose
  • Amount of credit: from 2 to 50 million soums
  • Credit term: from 12 to 48 months
  • Repayment frequency: every month
  • Repayment method: annuity - equal amounts after the same period of time
  • Securing a loan when applying for a loan in JOYDA: loan non-repayment risk insurance policy
  • Securing a loan when applying in the office: guarantee of an individual

Recipient requirements

  • Citizenship of the Republic of Uzbekistan;
  • Age from 18 to 65 years;
  • Official income for the last 6 months, old-age pension, 10% of the main account of the individual entrepreneur (minimum 6 months);
  • The debt burden on all issued loans and guarantee obligations should not exceed 50% of the average monthly income;
  • Absence of overdue debt on previously received loans and other obligations.
