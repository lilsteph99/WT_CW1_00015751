      Your money transfer will be accepted in the shortest possible time and anywhere in the world.
      
      The advantages of SWIFT money transfers are guarantees, security, and the ability to transfer large amounts of money compared to other international money transfers:
      without restrictions on the amount of the money transfer (except for the cases stipulated by the legislation of a particular country);
      
      send and receive money in any currency;
      
      can be used at any bank branch.
      
      
      When making a money transfer , it is important to correctly indicate the details of the intermediary banks involved in the transaction:
      SWIFT code of the bank from which the money is to be received;
      
      full name of the payee (natural person) or full name of a legal entity;
      
      16-digit recipient's account number (IBAN format);
      
      the name of the bank branch where the money is received;
      
      requisites of intermediary banking institutions.
      
      
      The term of making a money transfer using the SWIFT system is from 1 to 3 days.
      
      Choose major financial institutions as sending and receiving banks - in this case, the transaction can take only a few hours.
      
      It doesn't matter how much money you send - the commission for the service does not depend on the amount of the transfer!
      
      Transfer of money from the client's demand and other savings accounts and bank cards outside the Republic via SWIFT:  0.25% of the amount (incl. VAT)
      
      Making changes to the information in the consignment note/refund based on the client's application:  450,000 soums (incl. VAT)
      
      The amount of the service fee shall be at least 450,000 soums (including VAT) and no more than 4,000,000 soums (including VAT).
